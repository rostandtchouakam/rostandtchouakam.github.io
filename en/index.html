What I Do

Research Interests

Bridging the gap between economic theory and computational methods to solve real-world financial problems.

Financial Econometrics

Optimal consumption and portfolio decisions in continuous time under uncertainty, solving high-dimensional Hamilton–Jacobi–Bellman equations with machine learning.

Machine Learning & Deep Learning

Deep learning surrogates for improving econometric inference, particularly in generalized method of moments and M-estimation frameworks.

Theory Meets Practice

Embedding economic structure into neural networks to solve dynamic programming problems, connecting rigorous theory with practical computation.

Knowledge Sharing

Passionate about making quantitative finance and econometrics accessible through teaching and the Econometrica Learnia YouTube channel.
